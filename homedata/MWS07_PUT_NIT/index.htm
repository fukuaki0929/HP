THE WORKSHOP was founded by Prof. Krzysztof Kozlowski, Poznan University of Technology (PUT), and Prof. Hiroyuki Ukai, Nagoya Institute of Technology (NIT), under the academic exchange and cooperation program between Faculty of Computing Science and Management (CSM), PUT and Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), NIT, contracted in 2006. The workshop is focused on the interdisciplinary exchange of knowledge and experience in various fields on robot motion and control.
